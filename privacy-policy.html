Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2025
In Short: ContentFlow respects your privacy. We only collect data necessary to provide our scheduling service and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ContentFlow ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our content scheduling service. By using ContentFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose How We Get It
Email Address Account creation, notifications You provide during registration
TikTok Username Connect your TikTok account OAuth authorization
Video Files To schedule and publish You upload
Video Metadata Captions, hashtags, descriptions You provide

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

  • OAuth Tokens: Securely encrypted access tokens from TikTok
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the Service (features used, time spent)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: For authentication and preferences

2.3 Information from TikTok

When you authorize ContentFlow, we receive from TikTok:

  • Your TikTok profile information (username, profile picture)
  • Video upload capabilities
  • Basic analytics (views, likes - if you grant permission)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the Service: Schedule and publish videos to your TikTok account
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account
  • Communication: Send service updates, notifications about scheduled posts
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

Important: We DO NOT sell your personal information to anyone.

We only share your information in these limited circumstances:

4.1 With TikTok

We share video content and metadata with TikTok only for the purpose of publishing your scheduled content. This is done through official TikTok APIs with your explicit authorization.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share data with third-party service providers who help us operate, such as:

  • Cloud storage providers (for temporary video storage)
  • Analytics services (anonymized usage data only)
  • Email service providers (for notifications)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect user safety

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls with authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Token Security: OAuth tokens stored with encryption, never logged

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Videos: Deleted immediately after successful posting (or 7 days for scheduled posts)
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data retained indefinitely
  • Logs: Security logs retained for 90 days
